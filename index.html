Welcome to DocDynamo

DocDynamo is your AI-powered documentation assistant built to simplify prompt writing and maximizing quality of response generation. This guide will help you start using the Android version effectively.

Getting Started

  1. Download the official app from the Play Store.
  2. Open the DocDynamo Application and Upload your first document from which you want to get a response.
  3. Type the Query you are having and select the persona i.e. student, researcher, etc, it means in what language you want your answer to be. Student-friendly, researcher-friendly,etc..
  4. Hit the SEND button and get the most appropriate and most relevant answer with minimal or zero hallucinations by our model.
  5. Generate important questions, important concepts or get the additional information from the database within just one-click. Now no long prompting needed to get relevant response.
